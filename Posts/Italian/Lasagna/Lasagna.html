Ingredients for the Ultimate Italian Lasagna

Category Ingredients
Meat Sauce
  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb (225g) Italian sausage (or extra beef)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp sugar (to balance the acidity)
Ricotta Cheese Layer
  • 1 tub (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
Pasta and Cheese Topping
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step-by-Step Guide to Making Italian Lasagna

Step 1: Preparing the Rich Meat Sauce

  1. Heat a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add ground beef and Italian sausage. Cook until browned, breaking it up as you go.
  3. Toss in the chopped onion and minced garlic. Saut√© until the onion turns soft.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
  5. Add oregano, basil, salt, pepper, and sugar. Stir well.
  6. Let it simmer for at least 30 minutes to deepen the flavors. (Pro tip: The longer, the better!)

Step 2: Cooking the Lasagna Noodles

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Drain and lay them flat on a baking sheet to prevent sticking.

Step 3: Mixing the Ricotta Cheese Filling

  1. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan, and parsley.
  2. Stir until it looks creamy and delicious.
  3. Try not to eat it all with a spoon (I see you!).

Step 4: Assembling the Lasagna Layers

  1. Preheat your oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
  3. Add a layer of noodles.
  4. Spread half of the ricotta mixture on top.
  5. Sprinkle with mozzarella.
  6. Repeat the layers: Sauce, noodles, ricotta, mozzarella.
  7. Finish with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.

Step 5: Baking to Perfection

  1. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  2. Remove the foil and bake for another 15-20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  3. Let it rest for at least 10 minutes before slicing. (Patience, my friends!)

Pro Tips for the Best Homemade Italian Lasagna

  • Use fresh ingredients. Fresh basil, quality cheese, and good tomatoes make a world of difference.
  • Let the sauce simmer longer. An extra 30 minutes will give it that deep, rich flavor.
  • Layer evenly. This ensures every bite is balanced.
  • Let it rest. Cutting too soon = lasagna landslide!
  • Make ahead and freeze. Perfect for busy weeknights.
